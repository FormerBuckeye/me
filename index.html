Penghui Zhang (张鹏辉)

Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Engineering at Arizona State University.

Currently affiliated with SEFCOM led by Dr. Gail-Joon Ahn, Dr. Adam Doupé, Dr. Yan Shoshitaishvili, Dr. Ruoyu (Fish) Wang, and Dr. Tiffany Bao.

My research fields focus on the analysis of anti-phishing ecosystem, including phishing behaviors, sophisticated evasion techniques in phishing, and phishing measurements. I am expanding my research interest to a broad range of topics such as underground forum and systems security.
